Diffusion-Driven Two-Stage Active Learning for Low-Budget Semantic Segmentation
Jeongin Kim, Wonho Bae, YouLee Han, Giyeong Oh, Youngjae Yu, Danica J. Sutherland, Junhyug Noh
NeurIPS, 2025
arXiv / Code

We propose a two-stage active learning framework for semantic segmentation under extreme budget constraints. Our method leverages pre-trained diffusion models for representative candidate selection and introduces a novel uncertainty score to pinpoint the most informative pixels for annotation.
